2. Description of Service

YurUp is a comprehensive shared-living management platform that:

  • Connects housemates and families to coordinate daily life.
  • Facilitates chore tracking, routine management, and grocery lists.
  • Enables expense splitting, receipt tracking, and financial settlements.
  • Provides internal chat messaging and a "Household Court" voting system to resolve disputes.
  • Offers a dedicated "Family Mode" for parental controls, kid chore verifications, and reward allowances.
